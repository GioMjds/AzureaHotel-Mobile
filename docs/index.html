Privacy Policy

Azurea Hotel Mobile Application

Last Updated: December 6, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Azurea Hotel ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Profile picture and personal preferences
  • Booking Information: Check-in/check-out dates, room preferences, number of guests, and special requests
  • Payment Information: Payment method details and transaction history (processed securely through PayMongo)

2.2 Identity Verification Information

Camera Permission Usage: We request camera access to allow you to capture photos of your valid government-issued ID for:

  • PWD (Person with Disability) discount verification
  • Senior Citizen discount verification
  • Identity confirmation for booking security

These images are securely stored and used solely for verification purposes.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, pages viewed, and interaction patterns
  • Push Notification Tokens: For sending booking confirmations and updates

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Process and manage your hotel room and area bookings
  • Verify your identity for applicable discounts (PWD, Senior Citizen)
  • Send booking confirmations, reminders, and updates
  • Process payments securely
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Camera and Photo Library Access

Our app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera (android.permission.CAMERA): To capture photos of your ID documents for discount verification
  • Photo Library Access: To allow you to upload existing photos of your ID or save QR codes and receipts

You can deny these permissions and still use basic app features, but discount verification will require ID submission.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure data transmission using HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Encrypted storage for sensitive information
  • Secure cloud storage via Cloudinary for images
  • Firebase Authentication for secure user sessions
  • Regular security audits and updates

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Payment Processors: PayMongo for secure payment processing
  • Cloud Services: Firebase (Google) for authentication and notifications, Cloudinary for image storage
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Booking records are kept for legal and accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.
